About Wanderlust

Our story, mission, and the team behind your unforgettable journeys

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate travelers, Wanderlust Adventures began with a simple mission: to create meaningful travel experiences that go beyond the typical tourist itinerary.

What started as a small operation organizing hiking trips in the Alps has grown into a global travel company with operations on six continents. Yet, we've never lost sight of our original values: authenticity, sustainability, and creating genuine connections between travelers and local communities.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 50,000 travelers discover the world in a more meaningful way, with a network of local guides and partners who share our passion for responsible tourism.

Our Mission

At Wanderlust, we believe travel has the power to change lives and make the world a better place. Our mission is threefold:

For Travelers: To create transformative experiences that expand horizons, challenge perspectives, and create lifelong memories.

For Local Communities: To ensure tourism benefits local economies while preserving cultural heritage and natural environments.

For the Planet: To operate sustainably and minimize our environmental impact through carbon offset programs and eco-friendly practices.
